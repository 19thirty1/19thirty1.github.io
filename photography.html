About Me



I was born among the big trees -Redwoods- of California, live among the mountains of Washington, but call the ocean home. Adventure is my calling. I spent one year working for Horizon Air so I could travel every weekend to new places trying to find the best biscuits & gravy. (The best biscuits & gravy is along George Parks Highway in Alaska - I forgot to take note of the mile marker-)


I love to collaborate with thinkers, and doers, and the generally quirky. I love to be taken on adventures, and to take others on adventures. I love sipping whiskey with friends on the beach. I love viewing the world from a tree top or cliff side. And I love everything about getting to know a person for the first time.


I find very few greater joys than taking the time to develop a relationship with a person well enough to photograph them as perfectly as I perceive them. My first photographs were of my siblings as a youngster, and I continue to photograph them to this day. The first time I owned a personal camera wasn't until my teen years, but I have since taken thousands of photographs of hundreds of people. Some weren't so great and some were beautiful, yet all of them were an amazing learning experience.


If you're interested in collaborating with me, then scroll down and find my contact information. I welcome any kind of message, and love making new friends.
